Ernie Chang

I am a first-year doctoral researcher at Saarland University. My research interests broadly lie in low-resource and multilingual natural language generation. I am interested in the application to human-in-the-loop scenarios as in machine teaching. Specifically, I work on the following areas:

  • Structured data (e.g. tables, graphs) to natural language generation
  • Low-Resource Neural Machine Translation
  • Cross-lingual Style Transfer
  • Summarization
